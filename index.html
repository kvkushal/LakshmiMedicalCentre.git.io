About Us

Lakshmi Medical Center, established in 1992, has been proudly serving the healthcare needs of the Jayanagar 4th T Block community in Bangalore for decades. We are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services beyond prescriptions.

With nearly three decades of dedicated service, our store has been a pillar of support for the community, providing essential medicines and healthcare products to our valued customers.

Located conveniently in the heart of Jayanagar, we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services beyond prescriptions. Our commitment to your well-being extends to our free home delivery service, ensuring that you have access to the medications you need from the comfort of your home.

At Lakshmi Medical Center, we understand that genuine care goes beyond medications. Our knowledgeable and friendly team is here to assist you in finding the right solutions for your health and wellness needs. With a customer-centered approach, we are honored to be your trusted healthcare partner.
